About Rezi

A modern mystic,
rooted in Slavic techniques

I've been doing metaphysics since 2005 — two decades of cartomancy, tarot readings, live events and workshops. Based in Prague, reading in English and Czech. I carry the old Slavic art of divination into Prague's modern wellness world — for those who want more than a tourist attraction: the truth.

It started when I was a teenager — tarot first, then oracle cards, runes, and an old Slavic divination method passed down through generations. Thousands of readings later, my style is precise, grounded and unmistakably my own. People book my sessions because they work.

I'm probably the most rational psychic you'll ever meet. Expect no vague talk and no empty promises — you'll get clarity even on things you didn't realise were unclear, and the truth told with kindness. I want you to thrive, so I'll tell you how things really are — grounded, but empowering.

How it works

A session with me is a mystical experience — a glimpse into what lies ahead and clarity where you need it most, with a touch of Slavic magic and kindness.

i.

Message me

Write to me on WhatsApp or Instagram and tell me what's on your mind.

ii.

We pick time & place

Prague centre, a boutique spa, a calm local café or a nice park — or online, live on a video call or as a recording. In English or Czech, whatever suits you.

iii.

You share your context

No need to write your whole story, but the more specific you are, the sharper the reading. No question? Let the cards speak for themselves.

iv.

I read the cards

You ask, the cards answer, I translate — honest, with empathy and a touch of humour.

v.

Let it settle

You don't have to understand everything at once — that's the biggest trap. Some things only click weeks later. And that's the beauty of it.

vi.

Come back whenever

Some come every few months, some regularly. Follow what feels right — my DMs stay open.

Questions

Before you ask the cards

What visitors usually ask before booking a tarot reading in Prague.

Do you offer tarot readings in English in Prague?

Yes. I read in fluent English (and Czech) — in person at boutique wellness locations, cafés, your hotel, or online (live on a video call or as a personal video recording). Book via WhatsApp +420 737 079 739.

Can I book a tarot reading as a tourist visiting Prague?

Absolutely — most of my clients are English-speaking visitors to Prague. Same-week bookings are usually possible via WhatsApp, and we can meet near your hotel or in the city centre.

How much does a tarot reading cost?

Online readings start at €40, individual in-person readings in Prague at €65, group readings at €35 per person, tarot rituals at €75 and event psychic hire at €110 per hour. The exact quote depends on length and location — message me on WhatsApp for details.

Can I hire a psychic for an event or party in Prague?

Yes. I'm available for hire as an event psychic and tarot reader for weddings, hen parties, birthday celebrations, corporate evenings and boutique events in Prague and across the Czech Republic.

Do you do group tarot readings?

Yes — group tarot sessions for friends, retreats and celebrations, where each guest receives their own individual reading within a shared session.

Do you read tarot online?

Yes — online, either live on a video call or as a personal video recording you can replay anytime, anywhere in the world, in English or Czech.

Do online readings really work?

They work beautifully. Us being in different places doesn't weaken the reading — energy and intention have no borders. The cards connect with you wherever you are.

It's my very first reading — what should I expect?

Nothing to be nervous about — I'll guide you through every step. You don't need to know anything or ask "the right way". My job is for you to leave with more calm and clarity, not a tangled head. First readings are surprisingly lovely.

What do you offer besides tarot readings?

Tarot rituals for intentions and life thresholds, herbs & flowers guidance rooted in herbalism, and Venus-inspired beauty, wellbeing and femininity sessions.
